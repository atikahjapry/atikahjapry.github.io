Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working together.

How do we get started?
Simply select a service and complete the booking form. I'll reach out within 24 hours to schedule an initial consultation call where we'll discuss your specific needs and define the project scope.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and credit card payments. For larger projects, payment can be split into milestones. A 50% deposit is typically required to begin work.
Do you work remotely or on-site?
I primarily work remotely, which allows me to serve clients globally. However, for certain projects requiring on-site presence (e.g., workshops, go-live support), I can travel to your location within the UK or internationally with travel expenses covered.
What's your typical project timeline?
Timelines vary by project complexity. A Payment Analytics Audit typically takes 2-3 weeks. ISO 20022 Migration projects can range from 2-6 months depending on scope. Training sessions are usually scheduled within 2 weeks of booking.
Do you sign NDAs and handle sensitive data securely?
Absolutely. Client confidentiality is paramount. I sign NDAs before any data is shared and follow strict data security protocols. All sensitive data is handled in accordance with GDPR and financial industry standards.

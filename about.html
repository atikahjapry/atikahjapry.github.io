My Expertise

My expertise spans the complete payment lifecycle, with particular strength in areas where data analytics meets operational excellence. I don't just understand the technical specifications I understand how payments flow through complex banking ecosystems and where the pain points typically emerge.

MT Message Analysis

Deep proficiency in MT103 (customer credit transfers), MT202 (financial institution transfers), MT799 (free format messages), and other SWIFT MT message types. Expert at parsing, validating, and troubleshooting message failures across various fields including ordering customer details, beneficiary information, and intermediary bank routing.

ISO 20022 Migration

Comprehensive experience guiding organizations through the ISO 20022 transition. This includes MX message structure analysis (pacs.008, pain.001, camt series), field mapping from MT to MX formats, data enrichment strategies, and ensuring business continuity during migration. I help teams understand not just the technical changes, but the business opportunities that structured data brings.

Payment Operations Analytics

End-to-end payment lifecycle analysis from initiation through clearing and settlement. Specializing in identifying bottlenecks, analyzing STP (straight-through processing) rates, monitoring transaction flows, exception handling analysis, and building dashboards that provide real-time operational visibility.

Reconciliation Excellence

Advanced reconciliation methodologies for SWIFT transactions, including nostro reconciliation, correspondent banking settlements, and multi-currency matching. Developed automated reconciliation frameworks that reduced manual effort by 40% while improving accuracy and reducing breaks.

AML & Compliance Analytics

Transaction monitoring and sanctions screening analytics, pattern detection for suspicious activity, compliance reporting automation, and regulatory change impact analysis. Experienced with FATF requirements, sanctions list screening (OFAC, EU, UN), and building models that balance compliance rigor with operational efficiency.

Data Architecture & Tools

Expertise in Microsoft Fabric for building scalable analytics solutions, SQL Server for transaction data analysis, Python for automation and advanced analytics, Power BI and Tableau for visualization, and cloud integration (Azure, AWS) for enterprise-scale payment systems.
